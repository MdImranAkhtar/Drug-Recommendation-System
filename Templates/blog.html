Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In today's tech-driven world, timely and accurate healthcare is crucial. To meet the growing demand for remote healthcare, we developed a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app using Flask and a Support Vector Classifier model. This app helps users identify potential illnesses by analyzing their reported symptoms.

The Problem

The project began with recognizing a common issue: People often experience symptoms and seek quick answers about their health, but it's challenging to differentiate between diseases with similar symptoms. Our goal was to create a convenient solution for users to input symptoms and receive potential diagnoses.

The Solution

We developed a web app where users can enter their symptoms. The app uses a pre-trained Support Vector Classifier model to predict the most likely disease based on these symptoms. Here's how it works:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through an easy-to-use interface, which supports a wide range of symptoms.
  2. Machine Learning Model: The app uses a Decision Tree Classifier trained on symptom and disease data to predict diseases.
  3. Prediction: The app provides instant predictions about potential health concerns.
  4. Additional Information: Users receive detailed information about the predicted disease, including descriptions, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for easy symptom input.
  • Accurate Predictions: Leveraging machine learning, our model provides accurate disease predictions based on the symptoms provided by the user.
  • Comprehensive Information: In-depth information on predicted diseases, including advice on precautions, medications, diet, and workouts.
  • ducational Content: The app also offers blog posts on various health topics to educate users.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App combines machine learning with healthcare information, providing users with quick and convenient health insights. This project aims to make healthcare more accessible and user-centric, contributing to better health awareness and well-being worldwide.
